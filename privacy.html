Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026

The short version: Pitchy analyzes sound entirely on your device. Your audio is never uploaded to us or anyone else, and we don't collect your personal information.

This Privacy Policy explains how the Pitchy app ("Pitchy", "we", "us") handles information when you use it. We built Pitchy to be private by design.

1. Audio and the microphone

Pitchy uses your device's microphone only while you are actively listening to detect chords, notes, and key. All of this analysis happens locally on your device.

  • Your audio is not recorded or stored unless you explicitly choose to save a session, in which case it is stored only on your device.
  • Your audio is never transmitted to our servers or to any third party.
  • You can revoke microphone access at any time in your device's Settings.

2. Information we do not collect

Pitchy does not require an account. We do not collect your name, email, contacts, location, photos, or audio recordings. We do not use third-party advertising or tracking SDKs.

3. Saved sessions and app data

Any sessions, history, or settings you create are stored locally on your device. If you have iCloud enabled, this data may be included in your own device backups, which are controlled by Apple and your iCloud settings — not by us.

4. Purchases and subscriptions

Subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. We never see or receive your payment details. Apple may share limited, aggregated transaction information with us in accordance with Apple's Privacy Policy.

5. Diagnostics

If we use Apple's standard crash and performance reporting, this data is provided by Apple in aggregated, anonymized form and cannot be used to identify you. We do not combine it with any personal data.

6. Children's privacy

Pitchy is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Your rights

Because we don't collect personal data, there is nothing for us to access, correct, or delete on our side. You remain in full control of any data stored on your device and can remove it by deleting your saved sessions or the app.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

9. Contact
