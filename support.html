Frequently asked questions

How does Pitchy detect chords and notes?

Pitchy listens through your microphone and analyzes the sound in real time, entirely on your device, to identify the notes, chords, and overall musical key. Nothing is sent to the internet.

Can it really find the key of my voice or any sound?

Yes — that's what makes Pitchy special. It estimates the dominant musical key from almost any sound source, including humming, singing, or instruments. Clean, steady sounds give the most accurate results.

Why isn't it perfect on noisy or complex music?

Detecting many notes at once from noisy, real-world audio is genuinely hard. Pitchy shows a confidence indicator so you always know how sure it is, and accuracy keeps improving with each update.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are handled by Apple. Open the Settings app on your device, tap your name, then Subscriptions, and select Pitchy to change or cancel. You can also manage it in the App Store under your account.

Is my audio private?

Completely. All analysis happens on your device and your audio is never uploaded or shared. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Pitchy can't hear anything — what should I do?

Make sure microphone access is enabled for Pitchy in your device's Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone, and that no other app is using the microphone.
